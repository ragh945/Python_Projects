 ***Project Description***:
 
The project aims to perform a comprehensive analysis of cricket team rankings to understand how teams have performed historically and identify any patterns or trends. It involves collecting datasets containing ranking data for Test, ODI, and T20I cricket, cleaning and preprocessing the data, performing exploratory data analysis, and visualizing the findings.

***Libraries Used***:
* Pandas: For data manipulation and analysis.
* NumPy: For numerical computing and array operations.
* Matplotlib and Seaborn: For data visualization.
* Regex : To extract common patterns from sequence of characters.
* BeautifulSoup : To pull data outof html code.


***Steps Involved***:
* Data Collection: Obtain datasets containing historical team rankings in Test, ODI, and T20I cricket from reliable sources such as ICC (International Cricket Council) or other cricket-related websites.
* Data Cleaning and Preprocessing: Clean the datasets to handle missing values, inconsistencies, and outliers. Convert data types if necessary, and preprocess the data for analysis.
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):
* Summary Statistics: Calculate descriptive statistics such as mean, median, standard deviation, etc., to understand the central tendency and dispersion of rankings.
* Trend Analysis: Plot the rankings over time to identify trends and changes in team performances.
* Correlation Analysis: Explore correlations between rankings in different formats and identify any relationships.
* Ranking Distribution: Visualize the distribution of rankings to understand the competitiveness of teams in each format.
* Top Performers: Identify top-performing teams based on historical rankings and analyze their performance over time.
* Geographical Analysis: Analyze rankings based on the geographical region of teams to understand regional strengths and weaknesses.
* Data Visualization: Use Matplotlib, Seaborn, and/or Plotly to create visualizations such as line plots, bar charts, heatmaps, scatter plots, etc., to effectively communicate insights derived from the data.
* Interpretation and Insights: Interpret the findings from the EDA to draw conclusions about team performances, trends, and factors influencing rankings in different formats of cricket.
* Documentation and Reporting: Document the analysis process, findings, and insights in a report or presentation format to communicate the results effectively.
* Future Work: Identify potential areas for further analysis or improvement, such as predictive modeling to forecast future rankings or sentiment analysis of cricket fans' reactions to team performances.
